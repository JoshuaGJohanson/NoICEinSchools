Overview

State context

Maine has a relatively small but growing immigrant and refugee community. Portland has been a leader in welcoming immigrants and refugees, including issuing a sanctuary city declaration. The state's Democratic government has generally been supportive of immigrant protections. ICE activity is limited compared to most states.

ICE activity

ICE activity in Maine is relatively low. Enforcement operations have occurred in Portland and in agricultural communities, but the scale is limited compared to states with larger immigrant populations.

School policies

Portland's policies limit local law enforcement cooperation with ICE. Portland Public Schools has worked to maintain welcoming environments. The state Department of Education has been supportive of inclusive school practices.

Notes for advocates

Maine's immigrant population includes significant refugee communities resettled in Portland, including Somali, Congolese, and Central American families. Portland schools serve a highly diverse population relative to the state's overall demographics. The state has faced political controversy over asylum seekers, but local support for immigrant families remains strong in urban areas.
