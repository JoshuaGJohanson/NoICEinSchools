Stay connected

Get updates about campaigns and actions in your area.

Sign up to hear about school board meetings, petitions, protests, and campaign developments near you. We will only contact you about things that are relevant to your district and community.

* Required fields. We will not share your information with third parties.

What you'll hear about

  • Campaigns — when a campaign launches or advances in your state or district
  • Petitions — when a petition is open and needs signatures in your area
  • School board meetings — upcoming meetings where you can show up and speak
  • Protests — organized demonstrations near you

Choosing All updates means you hear about everything as it happens. Choosing Important only means we reach out when there is a specific action you can take that matters — a board vote coming up, a petition closing, a protest being organized.
