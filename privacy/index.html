Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 31, 2026

No ICE in Schools ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide:

  • Email address when you sign up for updates
  • Optional preferences such as topics, district, city, state, or interests, if those fields are collected

Information collected automatically:

  • Basic website analytics and security information, such as page visits, browser type, device type, and approximate location
  • Technical information collected by hosting or website infrastructure providers

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information unless you choose to provide it.

2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Send email updates and alerts
  • Let people manage their subscription preferences
  • Share opportunities to take action
  • Improve the website and content
  • Protect the site from abuse and maintain security

We do not sell or rent personal information. We do not share personal information with immigration enforcement or law enforcement unless required by law.

3. Email Communications

We use Brevo to manage email subscriptions and send emails.

If you sign up for updates:

  • You may be asked to confirm your email address
  • You can unsubscribe at any time using the unsubscribe link in any email
  • You can update your preferences through the manage preferences page

4. Website Hosting and Analytics

We use Cloudflare and related website infrastructure services for hosting, security, performance, and analytics. These services may process technical data such as IP address, browser information, and page requests in order to deliver and protect the site and help us understand general website usage.

5. Third-Party Links

Our website may link to third-party services such as social media platforms or other external websites. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies, not ours.

6. Data Retention

We keep email subscription information until you unsubscribe, ask for deletion, or it is no longer needed. We may retain limited technical or analytics information as needed for security, performance, or aggregated reporting.

7. Your Choices

You can:

  • Unsubscribe from emails at any time
  • Update your email preferences on the manage preferences page
  • Request deletion of your subscription information

For privacy-related requests, contact: admin@noiceinschools.org

8. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

9. Children's Privacy

This website is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact: admin@noiceinschools.org
