Overview

State context

Rhode Island is a small state with a significant immigrant community, particularly in Providence. The state has generally taken a protective posture. Providence has sanctuary city policies. ICE enforcement activity occurs but is more limited than in larger states.

ICE activity

ICE activity in Rhode Island is moderate given the state's small size. Operations have targeted Providence and Central Falls, which have large immigrant communities.

School policies

Providence school district has worked to maintain welcoming environments. The state has generally not adopted comprehensive school-specific legislation, but Providence's local policies provide some protection.

Notes for advocates

Central Falls, Rhode Island, has one of the highest proportions of foreign-born residents of any city in New England and has been significantly affected by immigration enforcement. Providence has a large Latino community, including significant Central American and Cape Verdean populations. The state's small size means individual district policies have significant impact.
