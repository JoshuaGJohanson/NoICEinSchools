Overview

State context

Montana has a very small immigrant population and among the lowest ICE enforcement activity of any state. No specific school protection policies exist, but the issue has limited practical urgency given the small scale of the immigrant community.

ICE activity

ICE activity in Montana is very limited. Enforcement is primarily concentrated at ports of entry on the Canadian border.

School policies

No statewide or district-level school protection policies. The issue has not been a significant legislative or advocacy priority given the state's demographics.

Notes for advocates

Montana's immigrant population is small and dispersed. Native American communities in the state face different but related law enforcement and jurisdiction issues. The low scale of immigration enforcement does not eliminate the need for protective policies, but practical organizing focus has been minimal.
