Utah Resources & Partners

Organizations, networks, and contacts in Utah working to support immigrant communities and protect students. Whether you need immediate help, want to partner, or are organizing locally, start here.

🚨 Need Help Now

If someone is at risk of detention or ICE activity is happening:

  • La Red de Solidaridad (Salt Lake)
    Community rapid response network supporting families facing deportation
    📞 801-448-7245
  • Utah Immigration Collaborative Hotline
    Connects individuals to legal support across Utah
    📞 801-382-9027
  • United Way 211
    Dial 211 — multilingual support for housing, legal, and emergency services
  • ACLU of Utah
    acluutah.org — report civil rights violations, know-your-rights resources

🏛️ Immigrant & Refugee Organizations

  • Utah Immigrant Advocacy Coalition (UIAC) — policy advocacy & family preparedness
  • Comunidades Unidas — citizenship, DACA, organizing, leadership development
  • Utah Immigration Collaborative (UIC) — network of legal providers
  • Asian Association of Utah — refugee services, youth programs
  • International Rescue Committee — Salt Lake City — legal services & refugee support
  • Catholic Community Services of Utah — immigration legal services
  • Holy Cross Ministries — low-cost legal help & community support

⚖️ Legal Support & Deportation Defense

  • Utah Legal Services — free legal aid
  • Immigrant Legal Services
  • No Más (No More a Stranger) — immigration legal defense
  • Lutheran Family Services Rocky Mountains

🏙️ Government & Institutional Resources

  • Salt Lake City Office of Access & Belonging
  • Salt Lake County New Americans Initiative

🍎 Teacher, PTA & Education Organizations

  • Utah Education Association (UEA) — largest teacher union in Utah
  • Utah PTA — parent organizing network across districts
  • Utah School Boards Association — influences statewide policy norms

🎓 Student & Youth Organizations

  • Utah Youth for Progress (UYFP) — student-led civic engagement and social change
  • Latinos in Action — Utah-based high school program
  • High school multicultural clubs
  • Student government organizations

🤝 Ally Organizations

  • ACLU of Utah — civil rights advocacy and litigation
  • Equality Utah — legislative and organizing infrastructure
  • Faith Communities — Unitarian Universalist congregations, Catholic parishes, interfaith coalitions
