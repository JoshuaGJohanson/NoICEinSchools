Overview

State context

New Hampshire has a small but growing immigrant and refugee community. The state has not enacted comprehensive immigration enforcement protections. Manchester and Nashua have the largest immigrant communities in the state. ICE enforcement activity is moderate.

ICE activity

ICE activity in New Hampshire is moderate, with operations in the Manchester and Nashua metro areas. The state's location along the I-93 corridor between Boston and Montreal gives it significance for border enforcement.

School policies

No statewide school protection policy. Manchester and Nashua schools serve immigrant populations without formal warrant-based policies. Some school administrators have worked to maintain welcoming environments.

Notes for advocates

New Hampshire's immigrant community includes significant Latino, West African, and Asian populations concentrated in Manchester and Nashua. The state's relatively small scale means that individual school and district leadership decisions have significant impact.
